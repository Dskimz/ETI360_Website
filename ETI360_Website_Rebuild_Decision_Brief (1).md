# ETI360 Website Rebuild — Decision Brief

## 1. Document Control
- **Document:** ETI360 Marketing Website Rebuild Decision Brief  
- **Scope:** Public marketing website (code-based, WordPress exit)  
- **Status:** Draft v1 (decision-ready)  
- **Owner:** ETI360 Leadership  
- **Decision rule:** Any change impacting credibility, governance tone, or claim discipline must be explicit and approved.

---

## 2. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
The website exists to:
- Establish ETI360 as **decision infrastructure** for educational travel risk
- Communicate seriousness, rigor, and governance alignment
- Convert high-quality visitors into **context-led conversations**

### In Scope
- Website structure, pages, section system
- Visual and design concept
- Content constraints and governance rules
- CMS content model assumptions

### Out of Scope
- Post-launch iteration planning
- Pricing publication
- Long-form copywriting

---

## 3. Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives
- **Authority:** credible within seconds
- **Clarity:** visitors understand what ETI360 is and why it exists
- **Conversion:** inquiry to conversation, not demos or trials

### Secondary Objectives
- Demonstrate disciplined thinking via Insights
- Reinforce educational travel specificity without marketing theatrics

### Explicit Non-Goals
- Education marketing tone
- Feature-led SaaS positioning
- Emotional or crisis-driven persuasion

---

## 4. Positioning and Narrative Principles

### Core Positioning
ETI360 provides structured framing and documentation discipline for educational travel risk decisions.

### Narrative Principles
- Approach before application
- Structure before services
- Boundaries implied through language and outputs, not disclaimers

### Language Discipline
- Use verbs such as *structure, clarify, document, support*
- Avoid verbs such as *manage, ensure, guarantee, protect*

---

## 9. Design Concept

### Visual Philosophy
- Calm, analytical, board-ready
- Restraint as a credibility signal

### Color & Typography
- Neutral palette + one restrained accent
- One primary font family
- Clear hierarchy, generous spacing

### Motion
- Minimal, slow, functional only
- No parallax, counters, or animated dashboards

---

## 10. Imagery, Icons, and Graphics

### Educational Travel Representation
- Context over participants
- Environment over emotion
- Recognition over persuasion

### Approved Imagery
- Campuses (exteriors)
- Transit environments
- Urban/regional context
- Learning spaces without people in focus

### Disallowed Imagery
- Smiling students
- Lifestyle education marketing
- Crisis or tactical visuals

### Graphics
- Structured diagrams
- Process flows
- Simple tables and matrices

---

## 11. Content Governance

### Voice
- Calm, declarative, precise

### Claims Discipline
- No compliance or certification claims
- No implied risk transfer
- No outcome guarantees

### Review Gates
- Claim discipline check
- Governance tone check
- Terminology consistency check

---

## 12. CMS Governance (Assumed)

### Locked in Code
- Page templates
- Section modules
- Typography, spacing, color system

### Editable in CMS
- Headlines (character-limited)
- Body copy (word-limited)
- Insights content
- CTA labels (controlled list)

---

## Appendix A — Global Consistency Rules
- Approach precedes services
- Platform follows methodology
- Boundaries implied structurally
